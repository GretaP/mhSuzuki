Located in Boulder, CO, I love teaching and performing equally. I maintain a small but vibrant private cello studio and perform regularly with the Boulder Philharmonic and its Outreach String Quartet.

I enjoy talking about the cello, so feel free to contact me with any questions, comments, or just to say hello.
