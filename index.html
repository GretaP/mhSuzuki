Who are we?

location_on Mid-Hudson Valley, NY

supervised_user_circle Suzuki Teachers

music_note All instruments welcome

We are a group of Suzuki Music Teachers in the Mid-Hudson Valley Region working together to bring the heart and high standards of the Suzuki Method of education to our local communities. Our members are all teachers with bona-fide registered training in their instrument from the Suzuki Association of Americas (SAA) as well as one member also an SAA Teacher Trainer.

We collaborate together to provide performances and valuable musical education opportunities to our students to help ensure a positive character-building experience for students and their families in their musical journey. The activities of the Mid-Hudson Suzuki Music Collaborative directly reflect our ongoing commitment to continue Dr. Shinichi Suzuki’s mission to nurture beautiful hearts and individual abilities while enriching and supporting family structures through quality music education.

For more about our Mission visit the Support Us page.
