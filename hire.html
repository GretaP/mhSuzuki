Need a Cellist?

I often play in weddings and other formal events. I am also available for casual and outdoor events.

Hiring a cellist is not as simple as picking a time and place for me to play. When you hire me, you are not just hiring a cellist - you are also hiring a music planner. We will work together to find out what your needs are and how to meet them.

To find out more, head on over to my contact form and send me some info about your event.
